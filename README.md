# Opportunity Cost Calculator

**[Open the app →](https://logwet.github.io/opportunity-cost/)**

A free calculator that helps you think through a purchase before you make it. Enter what something costs today, and see what that money might be worth later — either as the thing you buy, or if you invested or saved the money instead.

Everything runs in your browser. There is no account, no sign-up, and nothing you enter is sent to a server.

## What is opportunity cost?

When you spend money on one thing, you give up the chance to use it for something else. This app puts a number on that trade-off: it compares the long-term value of **buying** something with the long-term value of **not buying** and putting the money to work another way (investing or saving).

You can also flip the view to see what the purchase itself might be worth over time — useful for assets that hold value or appreciate.

## How to use it

1. Enter the **purchase price** (or use the quick-add buttons).
2. Choose how the **asset** might change in value — depreciating (like a car), staying flat, or appreciating.
3. Choose whether you would otherwise **invest** or **save** the money, and adjust the expected returns if you like.
4. Set your **current age**, **retirement age**, and an **inflation** assumption.
5. Read the result at retirement, the year-by-year table, and the chart.

Use the display toggle to switch between **Opportunity Cost** (the difference between the two paths), **Worth of Asset**, and **Worth of Alternative**.

You can **save** scenarios to compare later, switch between **light and dark** mode, and reset everything if you want a fresh start.

## Install on your phone

Visit the app **once while online**, then add it to your home screen:

- **iPhone (Safari):** Share → **Add to Home Screen**
- **Android (Chrome):** Menu → **Install app** or **Add to Home screen**

After that, the calculator works offline like a regular app.

## Your privacy

All settings and saved projections stay **on your device only**. The app does not collect analytics or send your data anywhere. Use **Reset All Data** in the app to clear everything stored locally.
